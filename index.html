❓ How to play

😈 Hungry Customer — make it LATE

• Late order = FREE food. Run out the clock!

• Tap any road tile to place the selected trap (🚧 roadblock, 🛢️ oil…).

• Tap a trap again to remove it (partial refund).

• Buttons like 🌧️ rain and 🐶 dog act instantly. New traps unlock each level.

• He fights back: smashing walls, shortcuts, and full 💪 RAMPAGE when furious.

🛵 Delivery Hero — deliver ON TIME

• Swipe (or arrow/WASD keys) to steer at intersections.

• Hold 🔥 BOOST for speed — it drains coins.

• Muddy shortcuts are faster but have pop-up nails. Time them — a nail = 💥 flat tire!

💰 Coins

• Whoever wins the round banks coins. Spend them on traps, smashing, boosting and better vehicles.

• Broke? Watch a 📺 for +50.
